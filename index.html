Thermodynamics Learning Cards

A visual educational tool designed for undergraduate thermodynamics teaching, categorized by distinct functional color codes.

💡 Design Philosophy: Every card is thoughtfully structured with a real-life visual illustration, a clear conceptual explanation, and a specific industrial application. This design aims to bridge the gap between abstract thermodynamic theories and real-world engineering scenarios. By consistently connecting concepts to practical use cases, these cards help students cultivate an industry-ready mindset, giving them a meaningful perspective for their future careers and job searches.
📧 How to Use: If you would like to use these cards in your teaching, or request high-resolution print files, please send an email to: yige.sun@materials.ox.ac.uk

Cards designed by Yige Sun (University of Oxford), and blended by ChatGPT.
